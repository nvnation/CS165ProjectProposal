Cost of Care for Different Cancer Patients

The purpose of this project is to take a more in depth look at various cancers and the cost of care for these cancers. We will be looking through data that tells us the average life expectancy and survival rate after diagnosis as well as cost per year.

Data and Visualization Sources

  • 5 Year Survival Rate(CDC)
  • Median Survival Time(MacMillan Cancer Support)
  • Relative Survival by Year of Diagnosis for Various
    Races and Genders(National Cancer Institute)
  • Incidence Trends for Cancer by State, Gender(American Cancer Society)
  • Annualized Mean Net Costs of Care per Patient(National Cancer Institute)
  • National Costs for Cancer Care(National Cancer Institute)

Additional Links

  • Annual Report to the Nation 2017(Infographic)
  • Projections of the cost of cancer care in the United States: 2010-2020.(Research Paper)
